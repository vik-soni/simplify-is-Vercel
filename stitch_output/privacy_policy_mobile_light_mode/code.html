01

Data Minimalism

We operate on a strict principle of Data Minimalism. Simplify IS only stores information that you explicitly provide for the purpose of your security assessment.

No Passive Tracking

We perform no keystroke logging, no hidden tracking, and no screen recording of your sessions.

Explicit Ingestion

Only data necessary for framework analysis and maturity scoring is processed by our systems.

02

Encryption Standards

verified_user

End-to-End Integrity

Everything within the Simplify IS ecosystem is encrypted at rest and in transit. We utilise industry-standard AES-256 for storage and TLS 1.3 for all data movement.

03

Data Sharing Protocols

Cross-Organisation Isolation

There is absolute zero data sharing between different organisations. Your assessment data remains isolated within your specific organisational container.

Internal Accountability

Within an organisation, designated Administrators can see team responses to facilitate audit accountability and centralised progress tracking.

04

Data Retention

In alignment with our Terms of Service, data ingested into Simplify IS is retained as long as the user account remains active.
