1.0 Data Minimisation Protocols

Simplify IS adheres to a "Negative Collection" framework. If a piece of data does not directly contribute to the integrity of the archival process, our systems are programmed to automatically reject its ingestion. We do not use third-party analytics scripts or tracking pixels.

2.0 Zero-Knowledge Encryption

The Monolithic Archive utilizes end-to-end encryption (E2EE) using the XChaCha20-Poly1305 algorithm. Your master key is generated locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. Losing your key results in permanent loss of access, as we maintain no backdoors.

3.0 Non-Disclosure & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our infrastructure (such as encrypted storage providers), so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential and maintain equivalent security standards.
